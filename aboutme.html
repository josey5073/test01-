A little bit about me

Hi, I’m Josefine Korsgaard Guldbek, 22 years old, currently studying at UCL Odense to become a multimediadesigner. My days are a mix of learning, creating, and exploring the endless possibilities of visual storytelling.

I spend a lot of my time illustrating for people all around the world, bringing ideas to life through custom artwork. I also dedicate time to personal projects, where I can experiment, play, and fully explore my own creative visions.

I’ve always been drawn to aesthetics - anything from minimal, clean visuals to rich, vibrant worlds - and I love the challenge of making every project feel alive and intentional. For me, design and illustration aren’t just about visuals; they’re about creating experiences that feel complete and meaningful, no matter the style.
